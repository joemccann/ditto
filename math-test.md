## Display Math (Block)

The integral of a Gaussian:

$$\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-x^2} \, d x = \sqrt{\pi}$$

Maxwell's equation:

$$\nabla \cdot \mathbf{E} = \frac{\rho}{\epsilon_0}$$

The Pythagorean theorem:

$$a^2 + b^2 = c^2$$

Sum notation:

$$\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{1}{n^2} = \frac{\pi^2}{6}$$

## Fractions and Nested Expressions

$$\frac{d}{d x}\left(\frac{f(x)}{g(x)}\right) = \frac{f'(x)g(x) - f(x)g'(x)}{[g(x)]^2}$$

## Matrices

A 2×2 matrix:

$$\begin{pmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{pmatrix}$$

A bracket matrix:

$$\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix}$$

## Cases (Piecewise Functions)

$$f(x) = \begin{cases} x^2 & \text{if } x \geq 0 \\ -x & \text{if } x < 0 \end{cases}$$

## Operators and Relations

Inequalities: $a \leq b \geq c$ and $x \neq y$.

Set membership: $x \in \mathbb{R}$ and $A \subset B$.

Approximation: $\pi \approx 3.14159$.

Arrows: $f: A \rightarrow B$ and $x \mapsto f(x)$.
